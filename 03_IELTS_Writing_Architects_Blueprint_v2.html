⑦ Paraphrase Worksheet 50 real IELTS prompts

Paraphrasing is the first move of every Task 2 essay. Change vocabulary AND structure — not just synonyms. Write your paraphrase, tap Hint for clues, Reveal for two Band 7+ sample versions.

📘 The 3-step paraphrase method — expand
1. Swap key words

people → individuals · think → argue · important → vital · big → significant

2. Reshape the sentence

Turn "Some people think X" into "It is often argued that X" or use a subordinator: While / Although / Despite the fact that…

3. Keep the meaning

Never add new ideas or drop viewpoints. If the prompt has two sides — keep both in your paraphrase.
