Descriptor Guide

Fluency (FC): Focuses on speech rate, logical connectors, and hesitations.
Lexical Resource (LR): Focuses on vocabulary flexibility, paraphrasing, and idioms.
Grammar (GRA): Focuses on sentence complexity, structure diversity, and error frequency.
Pronunciation (PR): Focuses on phoneme clarity, stress patterns, and ease of understanding.
