📘 Grading Rubric Quick Guide

Band 8.0+

Speaks fluently with only occasional repetition; uses wide vocabulary vocabulary idiomatically; structures complex sentences accurately; easily understood.

Band 7.0

Speaks at length without noticeable effort; uses flexible vocabulary; has good sentence control; shows only minor pronunciation errors.

Band 6.0

Speaks at length but loses coherence at times due to occasional hesitation; has vocabulary adequate for topic; uses mixed simple and complex structures.

Band 5.0

Speaks with slow speech and frequent self-correction; uses limited vocabulary; produces simple sentences accurately but struggles with complex structures.
