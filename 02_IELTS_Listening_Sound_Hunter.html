③ The Golden 30-Second Pause

The most crucial moments in the test are when there's no audio playing. Before each set of questions, you get short pauses. Wasting these is the single biggest Band 6 mistake.

Move 1
Underline keywords

Names, places, numbers, nouns. These are your signposts in the audio.

Move 2
Predict answer type

Noun? Verb? Number? Name? Date? Predict the grammatical shape of the gap.

Move 3
Predict synonyms

Audio almost never uses the same words as the question. "Yearly" → "annually". "Disadvantages" → "drawbacks".

⑩ The 4 Unforgivable Sins

Band 7+ candidates don't lose silly marks. Here's the kill list.

Sin 1Ignoring instructions

"NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS." You write three. Mark lost — even if technically correct.

Sin 2Spelling & plurals

"Reasons" ≠ "reason". A spelling slip is a wrong answer. Be precise.

Sin 3Losing focus

If you miss one, forget it instantly and refocus. The audio doesn't wait.

Sin 4Messy handwriting

Use the 10-min transfer window (paper) to write neatly. Unreadable = wrong.
