② The 'Power-Up' System

For every new word you decide to learn, capture three things. Not just the meaning.

Step 1

Word Family

All forms: noun, verb, adjective, adverb. Lets you flex sentence structure.

Step 2

Synonyms + Collocations

Synonyms = swap words. Collocations = "word teams" that always travel together.

Step 3

IELTS Context
