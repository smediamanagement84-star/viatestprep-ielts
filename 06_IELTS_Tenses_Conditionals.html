③ The Two Advanced Flashes

You don't need these every sentence. Used once correctly, each one signals Band 7+ to the examiner.

Flash 1

Past Perfect — The Flashback

One job: describe an action that happened before another past action.

had + past participle
❌ Band 6: "I arrived at 8:05. The train left at 8:00."
✅ Band 7+: "When I arrived, the train had already left."

Best place: Speaking Part 2 storytelling.

Flash 2

Second Conditional — The "What if?"

One job: talk about hypothetical present/future situations.

If + past simple, would + base verb
❌ Band 6: "I am not the president. I cannot make laws."
✅ Band 7+: "If I were the president, I would make healthcare free for everyone."

Best place: Speaking Part 3 + Writing Task 2 solutions.
