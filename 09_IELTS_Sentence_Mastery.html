Sentence variety = Grammatical Range. Today you'll master 4 sentence types, the punctuation that goes with each, and the connectives that join clauses cleanly.

① Clauses — The Building Blocks

Independent Clause

A complete thought

Has a subject + verb. Can stand alone as a sentence.

The government invested in renewable energy.
Dependent Clause

An incomplete thought

Begins with a subordinator (because, although, when, if…) or a relative pronoun (who, which, that). Cannot stand alone.

because the country wanted to reduce its carbon footprint.
Combined

The government invested in renewable energy because the country wanted to reduce its carbon footprint.
